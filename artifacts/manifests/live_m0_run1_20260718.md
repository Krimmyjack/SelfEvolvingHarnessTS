## Scientific interpretation

This is not evidence that Harness evolution failed. It is evidence that the
real API/evaluator path, cache replay, information walls, four-view feedback,
pattern mining, and slow-Agent invocation worked, while the growth loop remained
untested because proposals could not cross the schema boundary. H0 was safe but
incapable: risk cases were not damaged, period cases correctly abstained into an
operator backlog, and available repair capacity was not captured.

The run also exposed three instrument defects: an underconstrained slow schema,
semantic no-op supply, and misleading level/missing observables. Run 2 is only
admissible after those defects and the editable negative-probe selection route
are repaired and the offline acceptance evidence is regenerated.
